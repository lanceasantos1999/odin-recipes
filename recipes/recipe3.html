Soupe à l'oignon

French onion soup is a soup usually based on meat stock and onions, and often served gratinéed with croutons or a larger piece of bread covered with cheese floating on top.

Ingredients

  • 250g (8.8 oz) onions
  • 25g (1 oz) flour
  • 2 L (2.1 qt) white consommé or water
  • Cheese
  • bread, sliced and dried
  • butter, for sautéing

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Finely slice the onions and then sauté them in butter until they are softened but translucent.

  2. Step 2:

    3. When nearly sautéed, sprinkle with flour, and stir a couple of times to combine.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Pour in the water or the consommé, then cook for 25 minutes.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Place a dried slice of bread into each plate or bowl large enough to hold it, then pour over with the soup.
